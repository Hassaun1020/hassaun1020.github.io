Problem Statement: Assignment organization

There is no time-effiecient way to condense and view important assignments and exam due dates.

Affinity diagram: Scheduling Application

Developing an easy-to-use scheduling application tailored to faculty and students requires a lot of forethoughton the developers' end and with the help of an affinity diagram, our team brainstormed potential features and obstacles of development.

Team Personas:Real life Problems

My persona is about Sam Hamilton, a married women with 4 kids, trying to make it easer on herself dealing with the kids School work and keeping the house in check. She needs a website, were she can track her kids assignments and know what their grades look like. By using our app she can know what assginments her kids have to do and when they need to be turned in.
